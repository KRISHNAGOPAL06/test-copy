about

Wizcraft Entertainment Agency Pvt. Ltd. is a global experiential marketing company, delivering impact driven brand experiences. With a legacy of over three decades, we integrate strategy, creativity and technology to deliver solutions that inspire.

Headquartered in Mumbai, Wizcraft has offices in Delhi, Bangalore, Hyderabad and Chennai, with international liaison offices and a global partner network. Our brand portfolio spans a wide range of industries and geographies, with 600+ global brands investing their trust in us.

Wizcraft has emerged as a pioneer in the space of In-Person and Virtual Events, Exhibitions, Brand Activation, Advertising, Television Production, Live Entertainment, Corporate Communication, Digital Marketing as well as Lead Generation. From historic concerts like Michael Jackson’s India tour in 1996, to the BMW Experience Tour; from Tata Motors and Toyota at Auto Expo, to TVCs and influencer activations for Hyundai. In today’s virtual age, we continue to set industry benchmarks with exclusive virtual media launches, movie trailer launches and product launches, like Amazon Great Indian Festival, Renault Kiger Global Reveal, Kenstar Business Meet and more. Our corporate clients partner with us to execute immersive and engaging virtual industry conferences, business meets and annual parties, including Microsoft, Google, Cipla, Swiggy, Lenovo, etc. Our data derived solutions and lead generation campaigns help brands like Red Hat, IBM and others effectively reach their target audience.

Wizcraft’s solutions and experiences not only set trends, but also drive business outcomes. With the belief that an event may be forgotten, but an experience will last a lifetime!
